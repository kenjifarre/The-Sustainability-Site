Simplified explanation of climate change.

We’ve probably all heard of climate change and how it’s the most pressing issue of our time, but what is it exactly and what is triggering it?

Cause

  • The biggest cause of this issue is greenhouse gases.

  • These gases trap heat in the atmosphere and have been concentrating since the industrial revolution.

  • Among the most prominent greenhouse gases are Carbon Dioxide (CO2), Methane (CH4) and Nitrous Oxide (N20).

Effects

  • Among the effets are an increase in global temperatures

  • Melting glaciers and poles, which in turn rise sea levels endangering a large population of people and species living by the shore.

  • Sudden changes in precipitation patters, hurricanes, droughts and heat waves.

Solutions

  • Use public transport. Most large cities are have great infrastructure for public transport and it saves you money!

  • Eat Smart. Try reduce your consumption of beef and switch to chicken or vegan products.

  • Use energy-efficient gadgets. Using these will prevent more greenhouse gas emissions and save you money. Some examples include compact flourecent lightbulbs and efficient battery chargers.
